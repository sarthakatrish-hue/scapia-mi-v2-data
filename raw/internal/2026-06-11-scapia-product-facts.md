# Scapia product & company facts — verified 2026-06-11

Internal citation stash for wiki/scapia.md. Every fact below was verified via

conflicted, both are recorded.

## Product mechanics (current, post Feb-2026 devaluation)

- Card: Scapia Credit Card, lifetime free (₹0 joining/annual), both variants.
  — CardMaven review (2026-05-17) https://cardmaven.in/scapia-credit-card-review/
  — Paisabazaar (2026-06-10) https://www.paisabazaar.com/federal-bank/scapia-credit-card/
- Issuers: Federal Bank (since June 2023) AND BOBCARD (Bank of Baroda subsidiary);
  third bank partner planned.
  — TechCrunch (2026-05-21) https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/20/indian-travel-fintech-scapia-more-than-doubles-valuation-to-over-500m-in-a-year/
  — BOBCARD official https://www.bobcard.co.in/credit-card-types/bobcard-scapia-credit-card
- Network: dual — Visa + RuPay variants on one credit line/statement; RuPay
  variant enables UPI. (Visa tier "Signature" = secondary sources only, MEDIUM confidence.)
  — TechCrunch (2026-05-21), CardMaven (2026-05-17)
- Coin earn: 20 coins/₹100 on travel booked in Scapia app (4% value);
  10 coins/₹100 other Visa spends (2%); 5 coins/₹100 UPI via RuPay (1%);
  0 coins on international spends.
  — CardMaven (2026-05-17), SaveSage (2026-01-31) https://savesage.club/blogs/scapia-credit-card-updates-2026,
    CardExpert (2025-11-04) https://www.cardexpert.in/scapia-federal-credit-card-review/
- Coin value: 5 Scapia Coins = ₹1 (₹0.20/coin). Redeemable in-app on flights,
  hotels, trains, buses, visas, Scapia Store; up to 100% of booking; no
  cashback/statement credit; 36-month validity; may expire after 365 days
  of account inactivity.
  — CardMaven (2026-05-17), CardExpert (2025-11-04)
- Forex: 0% markup on international spends (unchanged by Feb 2026 devaluation).
  — CardMaven (2026-05-17); CardMaven forum (2026-01-27)
    https://cardmaven.in/forum/threads/scapia-devaluation-2026-scapia-lounge-access-spend-hiked-to-%E2%82%B920k.24717/
- Lounge: unlimited DOMESTIC lounge access on ₹20,000 spend in previous
  billing cycle (combined across Visa+RuPay; rent/utilities/insurance/fuel
  excluded from milestone). NO international lounge access.
  — SaveSage (2026-01-31), CardMaven (2026-05-17)
- Airport Privileges alternative: 100% value back in coins on airport
  dining/shopping/spa with boarding pass upload — ₹1,000/trip domestic,
  ₹2,000/trip international (same ₹20K milestone). International Airport
  Privileges: international flight booking ≥₹50,000 in-app → up to ₹2,000
  value back per booking. ~1/3 of users prefer privileges over lounges.
  — CardMaven (2026-05-17), SaveSage (2026-01-31), TechCrunch (2026-05-21)
- Reward exclusions: insurance (MCC 6300) and utilities (MCC 4900/4812/4814/
  4816/4899) earn nothing (post Feb-2026); fuel earns nothing (1% surcharge
  waiver — cap CONFLICT: ₹500/month per CardMaven 2026-05 vs "up to ₹5,000/txn"
  per CardExpert 2025-11); rent & wallet loads: 1% fee + GST, no rewards.
  Education/gift cards/government listed as excluded by CardExpert (2025-11,
  pre-devaluation source — MEDIUM confidence).
- Feb 2026 devaluation (effective 2026-02-27): lounge milestone ₹10K → ₹20K/
  month; insurance & utilities → zero rewards; International Airport
  Privileges added. Unchanged: 0% forex, lifetime free, earn structure,
  coin value.
  — CardMaven forum (2026-01-27), SaveSage (2026-01-31),
    CardInsider https://cardinsider.com/blog/scapia-federal-credit-card-major-devaluation/
- Eligibility (aggregator-sourced, MEDIUM confidence): age 21–65 salaried /
  25–65 self-employed; income ~₹35,000/month; CRIF 720+ (BankKaro/MyMoneyMantra
  2026) vs CIBIL 750+ helps (CardExpert 2025-11). Pin-code serviceability
  limits exist (anecdotal). Issuer-overlap policy fluid (existing Federal/
  BOBCARD cardholders may be ineligible per CardMaven 2026-05).
- Onboarding: fully app-based, video KYC, same-day approval typical, instant
  virtual card, physical ~3 days. — CardExpert (2025-11), CardMaven (2026-05)

## Company

- Founder: Anil Goteti (ex-SVP Flipkart). Founded 2022, HQ Bengaluru,
  ~250 employees (May 2026). — TechCrunch (2026-05-21)
- Funding: Seed Jun 2023 $9M (Matrix/Z47, Tanglin, 3STATE); Series A Nov 2023
  $23M (Elevation); Series B Apr 2025 $40M (Peak XV, ~$200M valuation);
  Series C May 2026 $63M led by General Catalyst (₹474Cr) with Peak XV + Z47,
  post-money >$500M (₹5,122Cr ≈ $539M, GC ~9.2%). Total raised $126M.
  — TechCrunch (2026-05-21), Entrackr https://entrackr.com/decoding/decoding-scapias-series-c-valuation-jumps-3x-general-catalyst-acquires-92-stake-11900055,
    Business Standard (2026-05-21), Inc42 (seed, Series A), Scapia newsroom (Series B)
- Scale: only relative figures public (May 2026): flight bookings ~6x YoY,
  hotels ~8x YoY, customers ~7x YoY, tier-2/3 share growing. NO absolute
  cards/users/GMV figures disclosed. — TechCrunch (2026-05-21)
- Federal Bank partnership: launched June 2023 (PR Newswire). NOT exclusive
  de facto (BOBCARD added; third planned). Exclusivity contract terms not
  public. Add-on card launched with Federal Bank (Scapia newsroom, date n/a).
