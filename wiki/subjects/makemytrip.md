## Coverage

### MMT Taps OpenAI for Myra — 2026-07-01

MakeMyTrip announced a partnership with OpenAI to power new trip-planning features inside Myra, its AI assistant, using OpenAI's APIs anchored in MMT's proprietary inventory and pricing data [^mmt-openai-2026-07-01]. The stated goal is to move travelers from conversational discovery directly to confirmed bookings within the MMT app — collapsing a funnel step that currently benefits card-layer players like Scapia [^mmt-openai-2026-07-01]. MMT's co-founder and group CEO Rajesh Magow framed this as "transforming curiosity into confident decisions" at scale [^mmt-openai-2026-07-01].

For Scapia, this is a material escalation: MMT now pairs social-commerce top-of-funnel (Creator Circle via Meta) with an AI-powered conversion layer (Myra via OpenAI), tightening OTA loyalty at both ends of the booking journey [^mmt-creator-2026-06-29] [^mmt-openai-2026-07-01]. Scapia's card-agnostic positioning remains valid — rewards still accrue on MMT bookings — but the risk is that a seamless inspiration-to-booking session inside MMT reduces the moment where a user thinks about which card to reach for [Inferred · Medium].

### MMT Launches Creator Circle — 2026-06-29

MakeMyTrip launched Creator Circle, a social-commerce affiliate program running natively on Instagram through a Meta partnership, weeks after rival Cleartrip launched its own Creators Club [^mmt-creator-2026-06-29]. MMT currently pays creators on engagement metrics and requires a 10,000-follower minimum, but has signalled a shift to confirmed-booking-based rewards within 6–9 months — acknowledging the direction while deferring outcome accountability [^mmt-creator-2026-06-29].

The twin launches reveal that creator-led travel commerce is moving from experiment to competitive battleground for Indian OTAs [^mmt-creator-2026-06-29]. For Scapia, the risk is structural: if MMT builds a durable social-to-booking pipeline anchored in Instagram, it deepens consumer loyalty inside the OTA ecosystem rather than the card layer. The card-on-top positioning remains valid short-term, but the window to develop a complementary creator or influencer strategy may be narrowing [Inferred · Medium].

### MakeMyTrip chooses measured AI — 2026-06-01

At its May 2026 earnings call — one of four major Indian travel company calls held within days of each other — MakeMyTrip set itself apart by emphasising measurable AI outcomes over transformative platform visions [^skift-ai-div3]. Where Ixigo pitched a ground-up redesign, MMT's management framed AI as a tool for demonstrable improvements in productivity and conversion, a more conservative but potentially faster-shipping posture.

For Scapia, MakeMyTrip remains the highest-reach OTA threat: its breadth across every travel category gives it daily consumer touchpoints that a credit card cannot replicate [^skift-ai-div3] [Inferred · High]. The strategic read is to maintain card-agnostic positioning — Scapia earns on MMT bookings too — while monitoring whether MMT's AI-driven conversion gains start to lock users into MMT's own loyalty ecosystem at Scapia's expense.
