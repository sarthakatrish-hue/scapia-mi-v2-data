## What we know

**Fuel excluded from base rewards** — Amex India stopped awarding Membership Rewards points on petrol, diesel, and CNG transactions from June 12 2025[^amex-deval-2026-06-25]

**High-frequency category targeted** — Fuel is traditionally among the highest-frequency spend categories for Indian cardholders, amplifying the impact of this exclusion[^amex-deval-2026-06-25]

## Coverage

### Amex Zeros Fuel Rewards — 2026-04-09

American Express India excluded petrol, diesel, and CNG transactions from its Membership Rewards base-earn structure from June 12 2025 — a significant cut given fuel is among the highest-frequency spend categories for Indian consumers[^amex-deval-2026-06-25]. Fuel spends retain milestone-counting status but yield no base points, a design that obscures the devaluation without fully reversing it.

For Scapia, this is a useful signal: even the premium end of the Indian card market is narrowing reward eligibility. Amex's move joins a broader 2025–26 industry pattern and gives Scapia a concrete contrast point — a travel-first card that hasn't stripped a core earning category after acquisition [Inferred · Medium].
