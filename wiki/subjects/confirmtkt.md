## Coverage

### ConfirmTkt Joins Ixigo AI Push — 2026-07-01

ConfirmTkt, Ixigo's train-booking platform, is included in the group's expanded OpenAI enterprise partnership — receiving autonomous agents, AI-assisted workflows, and contextual AI tools as part of a coordinated multi-modal AI upgrade alongside Ixigo flights and AbhiBus buses [^confirmtkt-openai-2026-07-01]. The upgrade is part of Ixigo CEO Aloke Bajpai's stated ambition to deploy agentic AI for over half a billion travel consumers [^confirmtkt-openai-2026-07-01].

For Scapia, train booking is a high-frequency category where card rewards could build habitual use — but agentic AI that automates the booking flow also automates the payment-credential decision [Inferred · Medium]. If ConfirmTkt's AI agents default to a stored card or UPI handle without surfacing alternatives, Scapia's reward value becomes irrelevant at that transaction moment regardless of its competitiveness [^confirmtkt-openai-2026-07-01] [Inferred · Medium].
