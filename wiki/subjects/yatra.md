## Coverage

### Yatra vague on AI plans — 2026-06-01

Yatra was the outlier among the four Indian travel companies that reported earnings in late May 2026 — not for a bold AI vision, but for the absence of one [^skift-ai-div4]. While MakeMyTrip cited measurable outcomes, Ixigo unveiled a platform redesign, and TBO at least articulated a productivity rationale, Yatra offered the least specific account of where it stands on AI and what comes next.

For Scapia, this signals low near-term competitive pressure from Yatra on the AI or product dimension [^skift-ai-div4] [Inferred · High]. The more productive competitive watch remains Ixigo and MakeMyTrip, where AI investment signals are clearer and the consumer-facing stakes for Scapia's card positioning are more direct.
