## Coverage

### HDFC Drops Small-Transaction SMS Alerts — 2026-06-24

HDFC Bank announced it will discontinue SMS alerts for small-value transactions from June 25 2026, a quiet cost-trimming step that reduces real-time spend visibility for customers who rely on SMS rather than app notifications[^hdfc-jun2026]. While operationally minor, the change sits alongside a broader 2026 pattern of India's largest card issuer tightening margins across its portfolio.

The move hands challengers like Scapia a small but concrete CX talking point: full, real-time transaction notifications regardless of amount. For frequent travellers making multiple small purchases — airport F&B, transit taps, in-flight purchases — uninterrupted alerts matter, and HDFC's rollback opens a gap. [Inferred · Medium]
