## What we know

**Annual fee & waiver** — Rs 2,500 plus GST joining and annual fee, waived on spends of Rs 4,00,000 per year.[^regalia26]
**Reward rate** — 4 reward points per Rs 150 spent; utility and telecom categories now carry a monthly cap.[^regalia26]
**Forex markup unchanged** — Markup stays at 3.5%, keeping it uncompetitive for international travel versus zero-markup cards.[^regalia26]
**Lounge access** — 12 domestic airport lounge visits per year included; no international lounge entitlement cited.[^regalia26]
**Welcome benefit** — 2,500 reward points on card activation.[^regalia26]
**Eligibility tightened to salaried** — Requires Rs 1,00,000/month salaried income or ITR above Rs 12,00,000 for self-employed; age 21–60.[^regalia26]
**Rewards cap signals devaluation** — Capping utility and telecom spends reduces effective earn rate for high-frequency bill-payers. [Inferred · High]

## Competitor Intel

### Where we win
**Zero forex markup** — Scapia charges 0% forex markup vs Regalia Gold's 3.5%, a decisive advantage for international travelers.[^regalia26]
**No annual fee** — Scapia carries no annual or joining fee, removing the Rs 2,500 barrier and spend-waiver pressure entirely.[^regalia26]
**Unlimited lounge access** — Scapia's RuPay-powered unlimited domestic lounge benefit exceeds Regalia Gold's capped 12 visits per year. [Inferred · High]
**Broader eligibility** — Regalia Gold's Rs 1,00,000/month salaried income threshold excludes a large segment of aspirational travelers that Scapia can serve.[^regalia26]

### Where they're ahead
**Brand & distribution** — HDFC Bank's scale and branch network give Regalia Gold instant trust and wide reach that Scapia cannot match today. [Inferred · High]
**Welcome reward points** — 2,500 reward points on joining provides an immediate tangible hook; Scapia's onboarding benefit differs in structure.[^regalia26]
**Mass-premium positioning** — HDFC explicitly targets the mass-premium salaried segment with a known, trusted brand, concentrating competitive pressure on Scapia's core audience.[^regalia26]

### What to do
**Lead on forex savings** — Quantify and push the 3.5% forex saving in acquisition campaigns; international travelers choosing between the two cards need this number front and center.
**Target lounge storytelling** — Reframe unlimited lounge visits against Regalia Gold's 12-visit cap in travel-creator and comparison content.
**Capture the income gap** — Market to aspirational travelers below the Rs 1,00,000/month salaried threshold that Regalia Gold now effectively locks out.
**Monitor rewards devaluation** — Regalia Gold's utility/telecom cap may trigger switcher intent; watch community sentiment and move fast with targeted win-back offers.
