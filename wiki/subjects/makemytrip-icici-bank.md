## Competitor Intel

### Where we win
**Unlimited lounge vs 3 visits** — Scapia's unlimited access dwarfs MMT ICICI's 1+2 annual allowance for any frequent flyer[^bttc-2026-06-26]
**Zero annual fee** — Scapia's ₹0 vs ₹999 removes the fee entirely[^bttc-2026-06-26]
**Zero forex** — Scapia's 0% beats even MMT ICICI's low 0.99% for heavy international spenders[^bttc-2026-06-26]

### Where they're ahead
**Lowest-income access** — ₹2.4L salaried threshold gives MMT ICICI the broadest addressable market; Scapia's eligibility criteria differ[^bttc-2026-06-26]
**OTA-integrated rewards** — MMT co-brand likely offers superior earn rates for MakeMyTrip bookings, reinforcing OTA stickiness Scapia cannot directly counter[^bttc-2026-06-26]
**Dual-network acceptance** — Two-network coverage reduces decline risk in markets where one network has weaker acceptance [Inferred · Medium]

### What to do
**Lounge differentiation at this tier** — At ₹999 fee, MMT ICICI's 3-visit lounge cap is the clearest gap; position Scapia's unlimited access as the upgrade story for travellers who've outgrown 3 visits[^bttc-2026-06-26]
**Target non-MMT bookers** — The co-brand's best rewards likely concentrate on MMT platform; position Scapia for travellers who book across multiple OTAs or direct[^bttc-2026-06-26]

## Coverage

### MMT ICICI Card First Profiled
A 2026 travel-card roundup introduces the MakeMyTrip ICICI Bank Credit Card to this wiki: ₹999 annual fee, 0.99% forex markup, ₹2.4L income threshold, and Priority Pass-backed lounge access at 1 international + 2 domestic visits[^bttc-2026-06-26]. The dual-network feature and MakeMyTrip co-brand integration position this as the lowest-barrier travel card in the comparison set.

The card's competitive threat to Scapia is real in the mass-accessible segment: 0.99% forex and a ₹999 fee make the cost case tight, though Scapia's 0% forex and unlimited lounge access remain decisive for frequent travellers[^bttc-2026-06-26]. The OTA co-brand lock-in is the structural concern — cardholders optimising for MMT rewards have less reason to hold a second travel card [Inferred · High].
