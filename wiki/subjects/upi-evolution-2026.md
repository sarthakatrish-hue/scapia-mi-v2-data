## What we know

**Credit on UPI live** — Banks can now offer credit lines via UPI, letting users pay on credit by scanning a QR code[^upi2026-stashfin]
**RBI raised UPI limits** — Higher transaction caps for hospitals and education unlock large-ticket use cases previously routed through cheques or bank transfers[^upi2026-stashfin]
**Global UPI QR expansion** — Indian travellers can use UPI in multiple countries, reducing reliance on forex cash or physical travel credit cards[^upi2026-stashfin]
**UPI Lite and Offline mode** — NFC and sound-wave-based offline payments extend UPI to low-connectivity rural and basement environments[^upi2026-stashfin]
**UPI Autopay scales** — Recurring-bill automation reduces late fees and builds habitual UPI engagement across subscriptions and utilities[^upi2026-stashfin]
**Voice and super-app features** — Voice-triggered payments and in-app gold/insurance/tickets broaden UPI beyond pure P2P and merchant payments[^upi2026-stashfin]
**UPI history enables SME lending** — NPCI's digital transaction record is now used by banks as a credit proxy for small merchants without formal paperwork[^upi2026-stashfin]

## Market Intel

**Direction:** Tailwind

**What's shifting:** UPI is expanding from a simple bank-to-bank transfer rail into a full-stack financial OS — credit delivery, offline payments, global acceptance, recurring mandates, and voice triggers are all converging in 2026[^upi2026-stashfin]. Credit on UPI, in particular, brings UPI into direct competition with credit-card swipe for everyday spending.

**Why it matters:** Scapia is a travel credit card. Its core value — rewards on card spend — is under mild structural pressure as credit-on-UPI lets cardholders transact without swiping the physical card, potentially bypassing card interchange and reward triggers. Simultaneously, UPI's global QR rollout competes with the forex-markup-free travel card proposition. However, the explosion of UPI adoption also validates the "digital-first" spending behaviour Scapia is built for, and credit-on-UPI requires a bank credit line — Federal Bank (Scapia's issuer) can originate these, keeping Scapia's ecosystem relevant[^upi2026-stashfin] [Inferred · Medium].

**Implication:** Scapia should monitor whether Federal Bank enables Credit on UPI for Scapia cardholders — if yes, it extends the card's utility to UPI-acceptance points; if not, a gap opens. On travel specifically, Scapia's superior forex rates and lounge access remain defensible advantages that UPI's global QR does not yet replicate for premium travellers [Inferred · High].

## Coverage

### UPI's 2026 Super-App Leap — 2026-06-24

UPI has crossed into full financial-OS territory in 2026, with the RBI raising large-ticket limits, NPCI rolling out Credit on UPI, offline/NFC payment modes, and cross-border QR acceptance now live in multiple countries[^upi2026-stashfin]. The shift from "balance-only" rails to credit-enabled, voice-triggered, and globally interoperable infrastructure represents the most significant structural change to India's payments stack since UPI's 2016 launch.

For Scapia, the double-edged read is real: Credit on UPI chips at card-swipe occasions, and global UPI QR nudges travellers away from forex travel cards[^upi2026-stashfin]. But the same credit-hungry, digitally fluent, mobile-first user who embraces these features is precisely Scapia's core customer — and premium lounge access, travel rewards, and concierge benefits remain outside UPI's scope today [Inferred · High].
