## Coverage

### Air India Maharaja Club Cards Coming — 2025-12-01

Air India signalled via a customer newsletter that new Maharaja Club co-branded credit cards are in the pipeline, expected to launch by March 2026[^aicc-2026-06-25]. The announcement comes as existing Air India Signature cards remain closed to new applicants and Vistara co-branded cards (including IndusInd's) are actively being retired and replaced with proprietary products — leaving a conspicuous gap in the airline loyalty card market[^aicc-2026-06-25].

The new cards are expected to serve both Air India and Star Alliance loyalists, and will coincide with an expansion of Maharaja Club points accrual and redemption to Air India Express flights — a meaningful improvement given the domestic short-haul network shift to the low-cost subsidiary[^aicc-2026-06-25]. For Scapia, this represents a near-term window to acquire premium travel card customers before a well-backed airline co-brand re-enters the market [Inferred · High].
