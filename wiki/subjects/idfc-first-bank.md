## What we know

**Card issuer** — Aggressive zero-forex play via Mayura + WOW! lineup; fastest growing card issuer[^oc-fy25-idfc-2026-06-22]
**OneCard primary issuer** — IDFC First Bank is one of three issuing partners for OneCard (FPL Technologies); described as a key co-brand partner[^oc-fy25-idfc-2026-06-22]
**RBI scrutiny via OneCard** — RBI reportedly asked OneCard's partner banks — including IDFC First Bank — to halt new co-branded card issuances pending clarity on data-sharing practices[^oc-fy25-idfc-2026-06-22]
**Club Vistara IDFC First discontinued** — Renewal fee, complimentary Club Vistara membership, and all ticket/upgrade vouchers discontinued from 1 April 2025 due to Air India–Club Vistara merger[^idfc-apr2025-cc]
**DreamFolks replaced by Elite Assist** — IDFC First Bank proactively migrated debit card lounge access from DreamFolks to Elite Assist; 45+ domestic lounges and quarterly visit entitlements unchanged[^df-lounge-idfc-2026-06-18]
**CEO in Amazon AI roundtable** — V. Vaidyanathan, MD and CEO of IDFC FIRST Bank, participated in Andy Jassy's India AI roundtable alongside Nykaa, Tata, and other senior executives — signalling IDFC First's interest in AI-driven banking infrastructure[^amzn-jassy-idfc-2026-06-24]

## Competitor Intel

### Where we win
**No co-brand programme collapse risk** — Scapia's Federal Bank partnership is stable; IDFC First Bank's Club Vistara card has had all its core benefits stripped, leaving cardholders with a fee waiver but no programme value[^idfc-apr2025-cc]
**Regulatory distance** — Scapia's sole issuing partner is Federal Bank; IDFC First Bank carries both the OneCard RBI data-sharing overhang and now a wound-down co-brand card portfolio[^oc-fy25-idfc-2026-06-22]

### Where they're ahead
**Distribution** — Branch network and pre-approved base dwarfs co-brand reach
**Multi-product issuing scale** — IDFC First issues its own cards (Mayura, WOW!) and co-brand cards for multiple partners; revenue diversification provides resilience[^oc-fy25-idfc-2026-06-22]

### What to do
**Target displaced Club Vistara IDFC cardholders** — These cardholders specifically selected a travel co-brand and now hold a hollowed-out product; they are natural Scapia acquisition targets with high intent[^idfc-apr2025-cc]
**Monitor RBI spillover** — If RBI's data-sharing action against IDFC First Bank's OneCard programme leads to broader issuing restrictions, it may slow IDFC First's own zero-forex card growth[^oc-fy25-idfc-2026-06-22]

## Coverage

### IDFC First CEO at Jassy Roundtable — 2026-06-24

V. Vaidyanathan, MD and CEO of IDFC FIRST Bank, was among the senior industry leaders who joined Amazon CEO Andy Jassy's India AI roundtable in Mumbai[^amzn-jassy-idfc-2026-06-24]. The participation signals IDFC First's active engagement with cloud and AI infrastructure at a leadership level — consistent with the bank's track record as an early adopter of fintech co-brand models.

For Scapia, the read is contextual: IDFC First is positioning itself at the intersection of AI and financial services at the highest level, even as it navigates the RBI co-brand scrutiny overhang on OneCard[^amzn-jassy-idfc-2026-06-24]. A deeper Amazon–IDFC First relationship — whether in cloud, AI-powered credit decisioning, or co-branded financial products — would represent a meaningful competitive development worth tracking. [Inferred · Low]

### IDFC First Swaps Lounge Provider — 2026-06-18

IDFC First Bank moved swiftly following DreamFolks Services' domestic lounge suspension, announcing Elite Assist as its new lounge access partner for debit card holders[^df-lounge-idfc-2026-06-18]. The bank maintained the same number of complimentary visits per quarter and walk-in access across 45+ domestic lounges, though web-based pre-bookings are temporarily unavailable during the transition.

The rapid pivot contrasts with peers IndusInd, Kotak, and YES Bank, which remained on DreamFolks with no announced replacement at the time of reporting[^df-lounge-idfc-2026-06-18]. IDFC First's proactive communication and continuity of benefit terms limits cardholder disruption — but the episode underscores the fragility of aggregator-dependent lounge models across India's card ecosystem. [Inferred · Medium]

### IDFC First Kills Vistara Benefits — 2025-04-01

IDFC First Bank discontinued the renewal fee, complimentary Club Vistara membership, and all ticket and upgrade vouchers on its Club Vistara IDFC FIRST Credit Card from 1 April 2025, citing the Air India–Club Vistara merger as the trigger[^idfc-apr2025-cc]. The card is effectively reduced to a bare credit facility — the three benefits that defined its travel value proposition are gone simultaneously.

The impact on cardholders is acute: unlike a gradual devaluation, this is a wholesale removal of the programme's core features in a single step[^idfc-apr2025-cc]. IDFC First Bank now joins Axis Bank and SBI Card in orphaning Vistara co-brand holders, concentrating a pool of travel-intent consumers who are actively without a home card — a targeted acquisition opportunity for Scapia. [Inferred · High]

### IDFC First Named in RBI Co-brand Probe — 2026-06-22

OneCard's FY25 disclosures name IDFC First Bank as one of its three issuing partners, and note that the RBI has asked these partner banks to pause new co-branded card issuances pending clarity on data-sharing practices [^oc-fy25-idfc-2026-06-22]. This is the first source directly linking IDFC First Bank to the same RBI co-brand regulatory action that has already been documented for Federal Bank.

The read for Scapia is contextual rather than direct: IDFC First Bank is a competitor in the zero-forex card space, and any regulatory friction on its co-brand operations is a mild tailwind for Scapia [Inferred · Low]. More importantly, it confirms the RBI's concern about fintech-bank data sharing is not isolated to Federal Bank's programmes — it spans issuers, which reinforces the need for Scapia to proactively audit its own data practices [Inferred · High].
