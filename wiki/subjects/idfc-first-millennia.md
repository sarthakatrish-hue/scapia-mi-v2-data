## Competitor Intel

### Where we win
**Unlimited lounge access** — Scapia's unlimited lounge access vs IDFC Select's 4 domestic + 2 international per quarter is a decisive win for frequent flyers who transit airports multiple times a month[^select-cardtrail-2026-06-28].
**No annual fee, no condition** — Scapia's ₹0 annual fee requires no spend threshold; IDFC Select's ₹999 fee is waived only at ₹2L annual spend, introducing a condition[^select-cardtrail-2026-06-28].
**Travel-native positioning** — Scapia's identity is built around travel rewards; IDFC Select's "best all-rounder" positioning is broader and less differentiated for travel-obsessed customers[Inferred · Medium].

### Where they're ahead
**Mainstream editorial endorsement** — Cardtrail's "recommend most often" label is the kind of organic, third-party validation that drives high-intent search conversions; Scapia does not hold this position in the guide[^select-cardtrail-2026-06-28].
**International lounge coverage** — 2 international lounge visits per quarter gives IDFC Select a credential Scapia must counter; frequent international travellers notice the difference[^select-cardtrail-2026-06-28].
**3X online rewards** — Elevated rewards on online spends (including international SaaS and OTA bookings) adds tangible value beyond zero forex that Scapia's rewards structure competes against[^select-cardtrail-2026-06-28].

### What to do
**Counter the editorial narrative** — IDFC FIRST Select's "best all-rounder" label in consumer guides is a positioning threat; Scapia should build a counter-narrative around unlimited lounge as a cleaner, unconditional value proposition vs quarterly caps[Inferred · High].
**Target the quarterly lounge-cap frustration** — Frequent flyers hitting IDFC Select's quarterly lounge cap are a high-value Scapia acquisition target; performance creative addressing "what happens after your 4th lounge visit" could be effective[Inferred · Medium].
**Monitor guide rankings over time** — Cardtrail and similar consumer guides drive significant card search traffic; securing a featured or top-ranked placement in this category is a meaningful SEO and brand objective for Scapia[Inferred · High].

## Coverage

### IDFC Select Tops 2026 Forex Guide
Cardtrail's 2026 zero-forex card guide names IDFC FIRST Select the card it "recommends most often," citing the combination of zero forex markup, 4 domestic and 2 international lounge visits per quarter, 3X rewards on online spends, and a ₹999 annual fee that is waived at ₹2L annual spend[^select-cardtrail-2026-06-28]. Cardtrail also verified the card carries no hidden cross-currency conversion fees, placing it in the genuinely-zero tier alongside Niyo Global and IDFC FIRST WOW! Black[^select-cardtrail-2026-06-28].

This editorial endorsement is the most strategically significant element for Scapia. IDFC FIRST Select is now the guide-recommended default for frequent international travellers who want a full travel package — the same audience Scapia targets[^select-cardtrail-2026-06-28]. Scapia's unlimited lounge access and zero annual fee with no spend condition are clear structural advantages, but they are not being reflected in mainstream consumer guides [Inferred · High]. Securing comparable editorial positioning should be a near-term priority.
