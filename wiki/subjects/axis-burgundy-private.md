## Coverage

### Burgundy Private Magnus Ratio Halved — 2026-04-02

Even Axis Bank's flagship ₹50,000-fee Burgundy Private cardholders were not spared the April 2026 devaluation sweep — the Magnus transfer ratio was cut from 5:4 to 5:2 effective 2 April 2026, halving the value delivered per EDGE Mile converted to partner programmes[^burg-apr2026]. Accor Live Limitless, Marriott Bonvoy, and Qatar Airways were simultaneously removed from Travel Edge for all cards including Burgundy, with no prior notice despite MITC requirements[^burg-apr2026].

The ultra-premium zero forex and unlimited lounge benefits remain intact, but the transfer programme damage is material for Burgundy Private holders who justified the ₹50,000 fee partly on the strength of premium partner redemptions[^burg-apr2026]. For Scapia, the signal is that no fee tier insulates cardholders from overnight benefit cuts at Axis — a positioning point that lands particularly well with premium-aspiring travellers evaluating their card choices.
