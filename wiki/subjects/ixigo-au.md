## What we know

**Direct Scapia competitor** — Lifetime free, zero forex, co-brand travel card aimed at the same user [^ixau1]
**Travel co-brand** — 10% discount on ixigo bookings; strong in trains and buses [^ixau1]
**Modest lounge** — 16 domestic (₹20K spend trigger) + 1 international / year via Priority Pass [^ixau1]
**Aggressive AI platform pivot** — Ixigo relaunched its app as "Ixigo NEXT," framing AI as a full platform redesign, not a feature add-on [^skift1]
**Conversational booking vision** — Users interact via natural language with an app that remembers preferences, replacing menus and filters [^skift1] [Inferred · High] this deepens the stickiness of the ixigo ecosystem that the co-brand card rides on

## Competitor Intel

### Where we win
**Zero forex edge** — Scapia's 0% markup beats this card on every international spend, a segment Ixigo NEXT's AI is not yet targeting [^ixau1]
**Premium travel positioning** — Scapia's Federal Bank partnership and Visa rails target aspirational international travelers; Ixigo's core strength remains domestic trains and buses [^skift1]

### Where they're ahead
**AI-native app experience** — "Ixigo NEXT" is the most ambitious AI transformation announced among Indian OTAs; conversational booking raises engagement and card stickiness [^skift1]
**Domestic mass-market depth** — Trains and buses give Ixigo daily touchpoints Scapia cannot match through a credit card alone [^skift1]

### What to do
**Monitor NEXT rollout** — Track whether conversational AI drives measurable card transaction volume growth; if it does, the co-brand threat intensifies materially [Inferred · Medium]
**Counter on international** — Reinforce Scapia's zero-forex and international lounge narrative in channels where Ixigo NEXT acquires users traveling abroad [Inferred · Medium]
