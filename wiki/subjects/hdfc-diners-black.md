## Coverage

### DCB Metal Specs Confirmed — 2026-06-26

A travel-card comparison roundup independently restates the HDFC Diners Club Black Metal Edition's core specs: ₹10,000 annual fee (waivable at ₹8L spend), 2% forex markup, and up to 10X SmartBuy rewards[^bttc-2026-06-26]. The notably low income threshold — ₹2,50,000/year salaried — is highlighted, making this a premium card accessible to a broader income band than its ₹10,000 fee might suggest.

No new product changes are present. The 2% forex remains the card's strongest competitive edge versus other premium peers; against Scapia's 0% it still adds material cost on heavy international spends [Inferred · High]. The unlimited Diners Club lounge access and Club Marriott welcome bundle continue to represent genuine premium depth[^bttc-2026-06-26].

## Competitor Intel

### Where we win
**Zero forex vs 2% markup** — Even DCB Metal's best-in-class 2% forex means ₹2,000 extra per ₹1,00,000 of international spend versus Scapia's 0%[^bttc-2026-06-26]
**Zero annual fee** — Scapia's ₹0 fee vs ₹10,000 (even with waiver requiring ₹8L spend) lowers entry barrier significantly[^bttc-2026-06-26]
**No earn complexity** — DCB Metal's multi-tier reward structure (5 pts base, 10X SmartBuy portal, quarterly milestone) adds friction; Scapia's model is simpler[^bttc-2026-06-26]

### Where they're ahead
**Unlimited global lounge access** — Diners Club unlimited domestic + international lounge access is a premium benefit Scapia's tiered model cannot match at zero fee[^bttc-2026-06-26]
**SmartBuy 10X rewards** — For HDFC SmartBuy-platform spenders, 10X rewards is a powerful earn accelerator unavailable on Scapia[^bttc-2026-06-26]
**Golf + lifestyle bundle** — Six complimentary golf games plus Club Marriott, Amazon Prime, and Swiggy One welcome package adds tangible lifestyle value[^bttc-2026-06-26]

### What to do
**Target forex-heavy travellers** — Even DCB Metal's 2% markup costs ₹20,000+ on ₹10L annual international spend; surface this in comparison content targeting frequent international travellers[^bttc-2026-06-26]
**SmartBuy dependency risk** — DCB Metal's best earn rate is portal-locked; position Scapia's open-earn model as more flexible for cardholders who don't channel spend through HDFC's own platform [Inferred · Medium]
